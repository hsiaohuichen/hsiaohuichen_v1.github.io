No 1 Overview

Setting Goals and Objectives

Project Brief
Device financing program offers affordable financing plans to purchase mobile phones in emerging markets.

The Problem
There are several roles in this program, including end-users, finance partners, and internal admin. For consumers, they need to check their own loan details, payment history, and make payment. For finance partners, they need a place to manage the financing plan on financed devices. For internal admins, they need to bind/unbind the financed devices for particular partners.

The Goal
Provide both client and server solutions for app and website.

Platform
Feature Phone (Client-side), Website (Server-side).
